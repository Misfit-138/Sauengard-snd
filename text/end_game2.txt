The party enters the inn, to the shouts and applause of a bustling and rowdy crowd from near and far, all jammed
into the confines of the bar.
Jenna works furiously to accommodate the patrons, as Tor'Bron's bellowing laughter and the smiles and cheers from all
fill the air.
Quietly retreating to the same old booth, you solemnly regard the throng and nurse your ale, in a mix of melancholy and
relief. Si'Kira approaches and slides across from you.
Sitting opposite one another, you clink your mugs and nod. The tumult becomes muted from your notice, and the two of you
smile with mutual fondness and share a drink together, enjoying unspoken words of admiration and friendship....
